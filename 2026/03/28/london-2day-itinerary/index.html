🇬🇧 London 2-Day Itinerary

整理自 Slack 群聊 | Original source: Slack group chat

🚂 Transportation: Gatwick Airport → Central London

From Crowne Plaza Gatwick Airport to British Museum:

  1. Walk or take a taxi to Gatwick Airport Station (5-10 min)
  2. Thameslink: Gatwick Airport → Farringdon (~45-50 min)
  3. Walk to the museum (~10-15 min)

Total: ~1h 10min

📍 Google Maps Routes

Day 1 (April 12): King’s Cross → Covent Garden → British Museum

### Day 2 (April 18): Paddington → Buckingham Palace → Big Ben → Westminster Abbey → Borough Market → Tate Modern → St James's Park → Heathrow Airport

🎯 Must-Visit Spots

Platform 9¾

  • Location: King’s Cross Station
  • How to find: Turn right after the ticket office, follow the signs
  • Perk: Staff helps with photos, free

Luggage Storage - Paddington

  • Official: £30/day
  • Third-party apps (Bounce): from £1.95, much cheaper

🗓️ Recommended Schedule

Day 1 | April 12

Time Activity
09:00 Leave hotel
09:55 Gatwick → King’s Cross
09:30-10:00 Platform 9¾ photos
10:05 Lunch at Covent Garden
13:45 British Museum
18:00 Return to hotel

Day 2 | April 18

Time Activity
09:00 Depart
09:30 Store luggage at Paddington
10:00 Buckingham Palace
11:00 Big Ben + Westminster Abbey
12:30 Lunch at Borough Market
14:00 Tate Modern
16:00 St James’s Park
18:00 Pick up luggage
19:00 Head to Heathrow Airport

⚠️ Notes

  • Borough Market: Most lively on Sat-Sun, some stalls closed on Mondays
  • Tate Modern: Closed on Mondays
  • Weather: London’s weather is unpredictable — always bring an umbrella!

🌆 South London Recommendations

If you have extra time, South London is worth exploring:

  • Battersea Park - Thames riverside, beautiful in spring
  • Brixton - Reggae music, Jamaican food, very local
  • Greenwich Park - World Heritage site, panoramic views of London
  • Brighton - Seaside town, ~1 hour from London

Enjoy your London trip! ✈️🇬🇧
