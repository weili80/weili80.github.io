🇬🇧 London 2-Day Itinerary

By Astra ·

Staying at Crowne Plaza Gatwick Airport. Here’s our 2-day plan.

📅 Day 1 | April 12, Sunday

King’s Cross → Covent Garden → British Museum

Time Activity
09:00 Leave hotel
09:55 Gatwick → King’s Cross (Thameslink, ~45-50 min)
09:30-10:00 🚂 Platform 9¾ (King’s Cross Station, turn right past the ticket office)
10:05 🍽️ Covent Garden - Lunch
13:45 🏛️ British Museum
18:00 🏨 Back to hotel
📍 Google Maps Route

📅 Day 2 | April 18, Saturday

Paddington → Buckingham Palace → Big Ben → Westminster Abbey → Borough Market → Tate Modern → St James’s Park → Heathrow Airport

Time Activity
09:00 Leave hotel
10:00 🚂 Paddington Station (Luggage storage)
10:30 👑 Buckingham Palace (Changing of the Guard 11:00)
12:00 🕰️ Big Ben & Westminster Abbey
13:30 🍽️ Borough Market - Lunch
15:30 🎨 Tate Modern
17:00 🌳 St James’s Park
18:00 🚂 Paddington - Collect luggage
19:00 ✈️ Heathrow Airport
📍 Google Maps Route

💡 Tips

🚂 Platform 9¾

Located inside King’s Cross Station. Turn right past the ticket office and follow the signs. Staff available for photos, free.

🧳 Luggage Storage

  • Official Luggage Point: £25-30/item/day
  • Bounce / Stasher App: £6-10/day (cheaper)

👑 Changing of the Guard

At Buckingham Palace, starts at 11:00. Arrive early for a good spot.

🍽️ Borough Market

Open Sat & Sun only (partial on weekdays). Lots of street food - come hungry!

🎨 Tate Modern

Closed on Mondays. Plan accordingly.

Have a great trip to London! 🇬🇧
